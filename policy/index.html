We do not collect any personal information or data from users of our App. This includes but is not limited to names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifiable information.

We do not collect any usage data. We do not track how you interact with our App, and we do not collect any analytics data.

Our App does not use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. We do not integrate with social media platforms, advertising networks, or analytics providers.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies to monitor your activity on our App.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal information, we do not implement data security measures related to personal data. Your use of the App is anonymous.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not target anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.
